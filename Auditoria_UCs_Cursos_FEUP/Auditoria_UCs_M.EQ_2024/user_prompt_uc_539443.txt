## Métodos de ensino e atividades de aprendizagem

## Componentes de Ocupação

- Frequência das aulas: 70.0 horas

## Fórmula de cálculo da classificação final
<p><span style="font-size: 7.5pt; line-height: 115%; font-family: Verdana, sans-serif; background-position: initial initial; background-repeat: initial initial;">A classificação final é calculada de acordo com a seguinte fórmula:</span><span style="font-size: 7.5pt; line-height: 115%; font-family: 'Verdana','sans-serif'; mso-fareast-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-ansi-language: PT; mso-fareast-language: EN-US; mso-bidi-language: AR-SA;"> <span style="background-position: initial initial; background-repeat: initial initial;"><br /> CF = 0,15 * T1 + 0,15 * T2 + 0.70 * EX</span> <span style="background-position: initial initial; background-repeat: initial initial;"><br /> em que T1 e T2 são as notas de cada teste e EX é a nota do exame.</span> <span style="background-position: initial initial; background-repeat: initial initial;"><br /> <br /> Está definido o seguinte critério de aprovação:</span> <span style="background-position: initial initial; background-repeat: initial initial;"> CF &gt;= 9.5</span>.</span></p>

## Programa
<p style="margin: 0cm 0cm 6.0pt 0cm;"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 7.5pt; font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">The course&#8217;s contente is organized according to the following topics:</span></p>

<p style="margin: 0cm 0cm 6.0pt 0cm;"><span style="font-size: 7.5pt; font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">&#8226; Concentrated parameters systems. 1st order systems; stationary gain and time constant. 2nd order systems; stationary gain, time constant and damping factor. Higher order systems.</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0cm 0cm 6.0pt 0cm;"><span style="font-size: 7.5pt; font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">&#8226; Distributed parameter systems.</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0cm 0cm 6.0pt 0cm;"><span style="font-size: 7.5pt; font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">&#8226; Complex systems. Identification.</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0cm 0cm 6.0pt 0cm;"><span style="font-size: 7.5pt; font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">&#8226; Frequency response analysis. Bode and Nyquist diagrams.</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0cm 0cm 6.0pt 0cm;"><span style="font-size: 7.5pt; font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">&#8226; Stabilty. Nonlinear systems. Nature of singular points. Application to Chemical  and Biological Engineering systems.</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0cm 0cm 6.0pt 0cm;"><span style="font-size: 7.5pt; font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">&#8226; Industrial instrumentation for process control: sensors and trsnmiters, controlers, final control elements.</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0cm 0cm 6.0pt 0cm;"><span style="font-size: 7.5pt; font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">&#8226; Closed control loop (feedback).</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0cm 0cm 6.0pt 0cm;"><span style="font-size: 7.5pt; font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">&#8226; Dynamic behavior o a closed loop system. Stability analysis.</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0cm 0cm 6.0pt 0cm;"><span style="font-size: 7.5pt; font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">&#8226; Control tunning: criteria and methods.</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0cm 0cm 6.0pt 0cm;"><span style="font-size: 7.5pt; font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">&#8226; Feedforward and ratio control.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 7.5pt; line-height: 115%; font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">&#8226; Digital systems for process monitoring, command and control. Treatment of analogic and digital signals.</span></p>

## Fórmula de cálculo da classificação final
<p><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 7.5pt; line-height: 115%; font-family: 'Verdana','sans-serif'; mso-ansi-language: EN-US;">The final grade is based on the following formula:</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom: .0001pt;"><span style="background-position: initial initial; background-repeat: initial initial;">CF = 0.15 * T1 + 0.15 * T2 + 0.70 * EX</span> </p>

<p> </p>

## Melhoria de classificação
