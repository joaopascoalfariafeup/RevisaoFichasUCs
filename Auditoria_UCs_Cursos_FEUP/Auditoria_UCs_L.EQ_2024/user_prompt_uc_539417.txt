## Métodos de ensino e atividades de aprendizagem
<p>1) Lectures (T) to introduce and explain the theory and solving some problems; <br />2) Lectures (TP) to solve and discuss proposed exercises; <br />3) Office-hours.</p>

## Tipo de avaliação
Distributed evaluation with final exam

## Componentes de Avaliação
- Exame: 70.0 %
- Teste: 30.0 %

## Componentes de Ocupação
- Estudo autónomo: 99.0 hours
- Frequência das aulas: 63.0 hours

## Fórmula de cálculo da classificação final
<p>Students can choose to be evaluated through a distributed evaluation system with a final exam or solely through a final exam.</p>
<p>The distributed evaluation includes:</p>
<ul>
<li>Theoretical-practical exercises (TPE)</li>
<li>Final exam (FE)</li>
</ul>
<p>The final grade (FG) will be calculated as follows: FG = 0.3*TPE + 0.7*FE</p>

<p>Students must follow FEUP's regulations concerning attainment of frequency.</p>
