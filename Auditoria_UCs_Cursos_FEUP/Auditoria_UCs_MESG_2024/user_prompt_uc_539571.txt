# Ficha a avaliar - versão em português

## Unidade curricular
Gestão de Operações e Logística de Serviços

## Curso responsável
Mestrado em Engenharia de Serviços e Gestão

## Créditos ECTS
6

## Ano
1

## Semestre
2

## Objetivos
<p>It is expected to provide the students with an understanding of the role of operations, logistics, and supply chain management in the services sector/industry. In particular, to develop the skills necessary to design, plan and manage operations and logistics in the service sector, considering the maximization of operational efficiency and client satisfaction.</p>
<div id="acfifjfajpekbmhmjppnmmjgmhjkildl" class="acfifjfajpekbmhmjppnmmjgmhjkildl"> </div>
<div id="acfifjfajpekbmhmjppnmmjgmhjkildl" class="acfifjfajpekbmhmjppnmmjgmhjkildl"> </div>
<div id="acfifjfajpekbmhmjppnmmjgmhjkildl" class="acfifjfajpekbmhmjppnmmjgmhjkildl"> </div>

## Resultados de aprendizagem e competências
<p>At the end of this course, students should be able to:</p>
<ul>
<li>understand the role of operations and logistics in the global strategy of the company;</li>
<li>design, plan, and manage operations and logistics in the service sector;</li>
<li>balance correctly operational efficiency with the service level and client satisfaction;</li>
<li>understand the need to align the logistic processes with those of the suppliers and clients.</li>
</ul>

## Programa
<p>Introduction and Basic Concepts</p>
<ul>
<li>Introduction to Operations Management</li>
<li>What do we mean by Service?</li>
<li>Process Fundamentals.</li>
<li>Operations Strategy</li>
<li>Process Selection &amp; Design (Services)</li>
</ul>
<p>Service Facilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Location Decisions</li>
<li>Design and Layouts</li>

</ul>

<ul>
<li>The origins of LEAN Production</li>
<li>The basic elements of LEAN</li>
<li>LEAN in services</li>
</ul>
<p>Capacity Management</p>
<ul>
<li>Service Capacity</li>
<li>Measuring Capacity</li>
<li>Capacity Strategies</li>
</ul>

<ul>

<li>Logistics and Transport Service Providers</li>
</ul>
<p>Supply Chain Management</p>
<ul>
<li>Supply Chain Integration</li>
<li>Pipeline Management</li>
<li>The Role of IT in SCM</li>
<li>Partnership and Collaboration</li>
<li>Service Level Agreements</li>
</ul>
<p>Facilitating Goods</p>
<ul>
<li>Inventory Management</li>
<li>Independent Demand Inventory Management Systems</li>
<li>Dependent Demand Inventory Management Systems</li>
</ul>
