## Semestre

- Trabalho escrito: 50.0 horas

5% em cada um de dois trabalhos individuais, 15% num trabalho em grupo de 2 alunos e 75% no exame final.

# Ficha a avaliar - versão em inglês

## Unidade curricular
Building Acoustics

## Curso responsável
Master in Integrated Building Design and Construction

## Créditos ECTS
3

## Ano
1

## Semestre
1

## Objetivos
<div style=&quot;text-align: justify;&quot;>This course aims to endow students with:</div><br /><div style=&quot;text-align: justify;&quot;>- Personal and professional skills, so that they can solve problems and conduct experiments efficiently&#894;</div><br /><div style=&quot;text-align: justify;&quot;>- Essential skills, so that they can design in this area&#894;</div><br /><div style=&quot;text-align: left;&quot;>- Essential knowledge on Acoustics, so that they can understand and correctly apply the Portuguese regulations in the area of Environmental Acoustics and Building Acoustics.</div>

## Resultados de aprendizagem e competências
-

## Pré-requisitos e co-requisitos
None

## Programa
<div style=&quot;text-align: justify;&quot;>1-Introduction to the course;</div><br /><div style=&quot;text-align: justify;&quot;>2-Basic concepts and terminology&#894;</div><br /><div style=&quot;text-align: justify;&quot;>3-Building Acoustics</div><br /><div style=&quot;text-align: justify;&quot;>- Acoustics correction<br /> (sound absorption, reverberation time)</div><br /><div style=&quot;text-align: justify;&quot;>- Sound isolation (airborne and impact noise)</div><br /><div style=&quot;text-align: justify;&quot;>- Installation and equipment noise</div><br /><div style=&quot;text-align: justify;&quot;>- Noise curves (NC, NR, NCB, RC)<br />- Speech intelligibility&#894;</div><br /><div style=&quot;text-align: justify;&quot;>4- Architectural acoustics<br />5&#8211;Noise in the Community</div><br /><div style=&quot;text-align: justify;&quot;>- Regulations (RGR, RRAE, etc);</div><br /><div style=&quot;text-align: justify;&quot;>6-Acoustic rehabilitation.</div><br /><div style=&quot;text-align: justify;&quot;> </div>

## Métodos de ensino e atividades de aprendizagem
<div style=&quot;text-align: justify;&quot;>This course is based on the oral presentation of the topics (with many audiovisual supports) in which examples of real situations will be given. Practical classes will be based on problem solving, practical experimentation as well as the application of regulations.</div>

## Tipo de avaliação
Distributed evaluation with final exam

## Componentes de Avaliação
- Exame: 75.0 %
- Trabalho escrito: 10.0 %
- Trabalho prático ou de projeto: 15.0 %

## Componentes de Ocupação
- Frequência das aulas: 50.0 hours
- Trabalho escrito: 50.0 hours

## Fórmula de cálculo da classificação final
5% individual work (x2); 15% work group (2 students); 75% final exam.
