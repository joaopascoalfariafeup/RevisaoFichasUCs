## Resultados de aprendizagem e competências
<p><span data-contrast="auto">Integration of knowledge and skills to carry out a piece of work on a specific topic in the course's area of knowledge, culminating in the presentation and public defense of a Dissertation.<br /><br />Demonstrate knowledge of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) established by the United Nations, integrating sustainability principles into Engineering practices. It includes being able to identify the contribution of student work to environmental, social and economic aspects, as well as defining performance indicators that establish metrics to measure the impact of projects, with a view to a more sustainable and inclusive future. If the student wishes to obtain more information on the topic, the following courses are suggested:<br />&#9679; https://dl4sd.org/course/view.php?id=4<br />&#9679; https://www.classcentral.com/course/global-sustainable-development-10311</span></p>

## Pré-requisitos e co-requisitos
Students should, whenever possible, complete this course on an exclusive basis. <br /><br />MIETE follows FEUP's dissertation regulations.

## Programa
Development of a dissertation, which can be carried out in a business or academic environment.

## Métodos de ensino e atividades de aprendizagem
During the development of their dissertation, students will have at least one academic supervisor, previously approved by the MIETE Scientific Committee.

## Tipo de avaliação
Avaliação por exame final

## Componentes de Avaliação
- Defesa pública de dissertação, de relatório de projeto ou estágio, ou de tese: 100.0 %

## Componentes de Ocupação
- Elaboração de relatório/dissertação/tese: 100.0 horas

## Fórmula de cálculo da classificação final
<p><span data-contrast="auto">The 2nd of June is the formal submission date for the MIETE Dissertation.</span><span data-ccp-props="{}"> </span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">However, anyone unable to hand it in on the&#8239;2nd of June&#8239;can do it until&#8239;18/July/2025.</span><span data-ccp-props="{}"> </span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">Submission of the dissertation&#8239;after the 18th of July&#8239;is subject to a special formal request.</span><span data-ccp-props="{}"> <br /><br />MIETE follows FEUP's dissertation regulations. <br /></span></p>

## Resultados de aprendizagem e competências
<p><span data-contrast="auto">Integration of knowledge and skills to carry out a piece of work on a specific topic in the course's area of knowledge, culminating in the presentation and public defense of a Dissertation.<br /><br /><br />Demonstrate knowledge of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) established by the United Nations, integrating sustainability principles into Engineering practices. It includes being able to identify the contribution of student work to environmental, social and economic aspects, as well as defining performance indicators that establish metrics to measure the impact of projects, with a view to a more sustainable and inclusive future. If the student wishes to obtain more information on the topic, the following courses are suggested:<br />&#9679; https://dl4sd.org/course/view.php?id=4<br />&#9679; https://www.classcentral.com/course/global-sustainable-development-10311<br /></span></p>

## Pré-requisitos e co-requisitos
Students should, whenever possible, complete this course on an exclusive basis. <br /><br />MIETE follows FEUP's dissertation regulations.

## Programa
Development of a dissertation, which can be carried out in a business or academic environment.

## Métodos de ensino e atividades de aprendizagem
During the development of their dissertation, students will have at least one academic supervisor, previously approved by the MIETE Scientific Committee.

## Tipo de avaliação
Evaluation with final exam

## Componentes de Avaliação
- Defesa pública de dissertação, de relatório de projeto ou estágio, ou de tese: 100.0 %

## Componentes de Ocupação
- Elaboração de relatório/dissertação/tese: 100.0 hours
