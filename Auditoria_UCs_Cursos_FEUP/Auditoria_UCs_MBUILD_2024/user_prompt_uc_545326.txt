# Ficha a avaliar - versão em português

## Unidade curricular
Conceitos e Estratégias para a Sustentabilidade (U.Porto)

## Curso responsável
Mestrado em Projeto, Construção e Gestão Sustentáveis do Ambiente Construído

## Créditos ECTS
6

## Ano
1

## Semestre
1

## Objetivos
This module aims to transmit the main concepts of sustainability and its various possible levels of approach for the construction sector. Frameworks like the UN Sustainable Development Goals, EU legislative and regulatory documents on sustainability and circular economy and the European Framework for Sustainable Buildings, Level(s) will be explored in order to understand the main concerns and the most adequate strategies to be adopted.

## Resultados de aprendizagem e competências
<u>LEARNING OUTCOMES AND COMPETENCES:</u><br />At the end of this module, the students should be able to:<br />&#9830;Master the main concepts of sustainability and to understand the various possible levels of approach;<br />&#9830;Be acquainted with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) and with how these goals could be pursued in the construction sector;<br />&#9830;Identify the most sustainable strategies and solutions to be applied in a building project;<br />&#9830;Know available methodologies and tools for assessing the sustainability level of those strategies and solutions.

## Pré-requisitos e co-requisitos
Basic knowledge about sustainable concepts in design, construction and management of buildings and infrastructures.

## Programa
<u>SYLLABUS/TOPICS:</u><br />1. What is Sustainability? The three pillars: environmental, economic and social sustainability. <br />2. The evolution of concepts and strategies over time. <br />3. The emergency of climate change. Implications. <br />4. Depletion of natural resources. <br />5. Sustainable Development Goals SDG. <br />6. The implementation of SDGs. Impact on the construction sector. <br />7. Affordable and clean energy. <br />8. Sustainable cities and communities. <br />9. Legislative and regulatory framework, the case of EU. <br />10. Strategies for a circular economy in building construction. <br />11. Materials sustainability: LCA fundamentals. <br />12. Levels: European Framework for Sustainable Buildings. <br />13. Case studies.

## Métodos de ensino e atividades de aprendizagem
Lectures and class-oriented development of courseworks, with individual research.

## Tipo de avaliação
Avaliação distribuída com exame final

## Componentes de Avaliação
- Apresentação/discussão de um trabalho científico: 5.0 %
- Exame: 50.0 %
- Trabalho prático ou de projeto: 40.0 %
- Participação presencial: 5.0 %

## Componentes de Ocupação
- Apresentação/discussão de um trabalho científico: 1.0 horas
- Estudo autónomo: 35.0 horas
- Frequência das aulas: 56.0 horas
- Trabalho de investigação: 35.0 horas
- Elaboração de projeto: 35.0 horas

## Fórmula de cálculo da classificação final
The module will be assessed (in two opportunities) by:<br />- A group coursework, to be developed during the teaching period;<br />- An exam, at the end of the teaching period.<br /><div style=&quot;padding-left: 30px;&quot;>&#9830; 50% Group Coursework<br />&#9830; 50% Exam<br />&#9830; &#931; = 100%</div>
