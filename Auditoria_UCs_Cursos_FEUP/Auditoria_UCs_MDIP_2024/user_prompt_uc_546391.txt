# Ficha a avaliar - versão em inglês

## Objetivos
<p><strong>Objectives. Learning Competences </strong></p>
<p>Integration of multidisciplinary expertise in creating new products  with the   objective of learning a set of strategies and tools for systematic development of new products, in an environment of teamwork. </p>

## Resultados de aprendizagem e competências
<p>It is intended that at the end of the course the student will acquire the following competences and be able to :&#8232;</p>
<ul>
<li>Define and enumerate new concepts such as incremental and radical innovation , context, rate of mortality of new projects</li>
<li>Define product planning versus company strategy</li>
<li>Discuss alignment of product planning versus company strategy</li>
<li>Develop and apply main tools of product planning : mission statement and product charter</li>
<li>Describe structured models of product development and name its historic perspective</li>
<li>Understand product/service development as a system through the adoption of holystic approaches</li>
<li>Understand importance of customer involvement in new product development and service co-creation</li>
<li>Use different tools to gather information</li>
<li>Classify and compare different needs/problems</li>
<li>Integrate data/information and translate them into a specification of the product/service</li>
<li>Discuss the use of functional analysis. Define different types of product architectures</li>
<li>Use different structured tools to optimize the search of creative solutions</li>
<li>Problem decomposition into simpler subsystems</li>
<li>Combine different solutions for different subsystems to obtain alternative concepts and product service architectures</li>
<li>Distinguish and understand the limitations of linear models of product development and importance of flexible processes that recognize dynamic nature of the context</li>
<li>Discuss and understand how to deal with uncertainty of the context and complexity of the product system</li>
<li>Apply different management strategies aligned with the business context</li>
<li>Define aggregate project plan</li>
<li>Discuss and understand the integrated nature of new product development in portfolios and programs</li>
<li>Use the aggregate project plan as a visualization tool to allocate resources and manage different types of innovation projects in a more efficient and effective way</li>
<li>Define and describe new philosophies of NPD : design thinking and lean thinking</li>
<li>Define new instruments to sense new ways of detecting discontinuities and new opportunities of innovating</li>
<li>Apply new tools of sensing opportunities : corporate entrepreneuship, organizational slack and collaborative networks</li>
<li>Discuss and understand the concept of customer experience and how to involve customers in the service co-creation</li>
<li>Apply tools of service design innovation : service concept , service architecture , service navigation and service blueprint</li>
<li>Develop a service prototype as a representation of a service concept before its materialization</li>
<li>Apply knowledge through the discussion of case studies depicting real business situations</li>
<li>Apply and synthetize knowledge through a team project, from the identification of a business opportunity to the generation of a concept and prototype</li>
</ul>

## Programa
<ol><br /><li>Introduction to the course content&#8232;and teaching methodology</li><br /><li>Concepts of innovation and design spaces</li><br /><li>Strategic product planning</li><br /><li>Structured models of development &#8232;</li><br /><li>Customer experience modelling and involvement &#8232;</li><br /><li>Concept generation and system architecture</li><br /><li>Flexible processes of product development</li><br /><li>Case studies </li><br /><li>Team project</li><br /></ol><br /><p> </p>

## Métodos de ensino e atividades de aprendizagem
<p>The UC uses a PBL-project based learning approach, based on the development of a project to create a new product or service corresponding to a business opportunity identified by the team (4-6 students) or proposed by a company, using the models and tools  taught in the course. Classes will comprise three components:</p>
<ul>
<li>Lectures on new product/service development concepts and methodologies.</li>
<li>Discussion of cases</li>
<li>Project follow-up and discussion, with intermediary and final presentations of new concept developed</li>
</ul>

## Tipo de avaliação
Distributed evaluation without final exam

## Componentes de Avaliação
- Participação presencial: 30.0 %
- Trabalho escrito: 70.0 %

## Componentes de Ocupação
- Elaboração de relatório/dissertação/tese: 119.0 hours
- Frequência das aulas: 42.0 hours

## Fórmula de cálculo da classificação final
<p>The final mark  is based on the following criteria:&#8232;</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>30 % </strong>Class attendance and participation on presentation and discussion of case studies and progress reports relating to assignments planned:</li>
<li><strong>70% </strong>Presentation and discussion of the final report of the project to develop an innovative product or service</li>
</ul>
