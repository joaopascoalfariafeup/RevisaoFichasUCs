# Ficha a avaliar - versão em português

## Unidade curricular
Métodos Avançados de  Discretização

## Curso responsável
Mestrado em Mecânica Computacional

## Créditos ECTS
7,5

## Ano
2

## Semestre
1

## Objetivos
Apprehension of the fundamental concepts of available advanced discretization techniques (meshless methods). Developing the ability to use the knowledge acquired to solve simple computational mechanics problems using meshless methods.

## Resultados de aprendizagem e competências
Capability to design simple structures and mechanical components.

## Pré-requisitos e co-requisitos
It is necessary to have basic knowledge in: Solid Mechanics, Approximation numerical techniques, Algebra, programming.

## Programa
<p>1-Brief introduction to Matlab.</p>
<p>2-Introduction to Advanced Discretization Techniques</p>
<p>  - Comparison with the Finite Element Method</p>
<p>  - Available Meshless methods</p>
<p>  - kinds of approximation/interpolation functions</p>
<p>  - influence domain concept</p>
<p>  - numeric integration schemes</p>
<p>3-Brief Presentation on Solid Mechanics</p>
<p>  - Hooke&#8217;s Law</p>
<p>  - Equilibrium Equations</p>
<p>  - Galerkin Weak Form</p>
<p>  - Discrete Equation System</p>
<p>4-Finite element method</p>
<p>- Formulation: Discretization, integration mesh, shape functions, matrix formulaiton</p>
<p>- Matlab programming 2D problems using linear triangular elements</p>
<p>- Matlab programming 2D problems using quadrilateral elements</p>
<p>5-Meshless Methods</p>
<p>  - Approximation Functions (Moving Least Square Approximants)</p>
<p>  - Interpolation Functions (Radial Point Interpolators)</p>
<p>  - Matrix Formulation</p>
<p>  - Essential and Natural Boundary imposition</p>
<p>6-Numeric Implementation</p>
<p>7-Basic Meshless Software in Matlab (open-source software created by the lecture)</p>
<p>  - Matlab introduction</p>
<p>  - Presentation of the several routines and subroutines of the meshless method software</p>
<p>  - Solving simple examples</p>
<p>  - Extending the software to other fields of computational mechanics</p>

## Métodos de ensino e atividades de aprendizagem
<p>Theoretical-Practical classes with exposition of the relevant theory.</p>
<p>Practical classes to solve typical problems at the end of each unit.</p>
<p>Suggestion of problems to be solved individually in class and at home.</p>
<p>It is delivered to all students a copy of a open-source meshless software created by the lecture.</p>

## Tipo de avaliação
Avaliação distribuída com exame final

## Componentes de Avaliação
- Defesa pública de dissertação, de relatório de projeto ou estágio, ou de tese: 10.0 %
- Exame: 40.0 %
- Trabalho escrito: 50.0 %

## Fórmula de cálculo da classificação final
<p>50% of final exam + 40% of the final report + 10% of the final presentation.</p>
<p>At the end of the semester all students will be evaluated with a final exam rated from 0 to 20 values. Final grades higher than 17 values are conditioned to an oral exam.</p>
